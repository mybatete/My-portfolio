Education

After Going to Colege for Two year at the Protestant University of Congo, I traveled to the United States of America to further my education. I left friends and family behind to engage in a perillous lonely adventure that was intimidating. Upon arriving at Lewiston In Idaho, I was conditionally accepted to Lewis-Clark State College, If I was able to learn english and be fluent enough to attend an American college. I thus started at the Institute of Intensive English(IIE). Initially expected to spend 3 semesters to achieve the needed proof of profficiency, I completed the program in only three fourth of the semester and was able to attend college Fall 2017. I received the certificate, and was given a choice. stay for one more semester and improve on my skills or take college on with brushwhood legs. I chose to take my chance, at which point I was faced with the choice of a major. After studying Business administration for two years, i was not convinved. I had a second chance, which is rare, and I was going to take full advantage of it. after thinking about it, I chose to major in Computer Science. Which I really much enjoyed and excelled at. I studied at Lewis-Clark State College for 4 years, and graduated with my Bachelor degree May of 2020.
